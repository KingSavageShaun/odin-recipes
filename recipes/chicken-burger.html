Chicken Burger

Home-made Chicken Burger

Description

This dish is created with use of an air fryer & blender. It is high in protein without being high in calories due to using lean parts of a chicken, in this case, a breast.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Burger bun of your choice (low sugar preferred)
  • Low fat mayo
  • Lettuce
  • One egg
  • Two rice crackers
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Garlic salt

Steps

  1. Get the egg whites from the egg & set aside of a flat plate
  2. Break up both crackers into a blender along with a dash of paprika, garlic powder & garlic salt
  3. Blend for about 10 seconds until all contents are a fine powder, then empty onto another plate
  4. Cut chicken breast into fillet shapes to go in the burger
  5. Begin to pre-heat the air fryer to 190 degrees celsius
  6. Take the chicke fillets, dip them into the egg whites, then cover them with the powder remains from the blender & set aside on a plate
  7. Once the air fryer is ready, put the fillets in for 15 minutes, flip at the halfway point
  8. Once finished, take the chicken out and put into the bun
  9. Use this point to add mayo, lettuce & any additional vegetables to the burger
  10. With all of the above completed, it is now ready to consume
